Number of Marginalized Community

India is home to a diverse array of marginalized communities, spread across various states and regions. These communities include Scheduled Castes (SCs), Scheduled Tribes (STs), and Other Backward Classes (OBCs), among others. The distribution of marginalized communities varies across states, with certain regions having a higher concentration of specific groups. Several factors contribute to the high concentration of marginalized communities such as historical practices, geographical factors, economic disparity, land rights and displacement and cultural diversity.
